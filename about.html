About Me

Hello! I'm Nat. For over two decades, I've been involved in the world of interaction and digital product design. My focus has always been on creating engaging experiences and helping to guide new talent in the field. I enjoy working on a variety of projects, including interactive installations, websites, and mobile applications.

I have experience leading design teams and projects, always aiming to find clear, effective solutions. My background includes co-founding a design studio earlier in my career, where I focused on creative technology.

What I Do Now

Currently, I work in a leadership role focusing on design and digital products. A significant part of my work involves guiding teams, developing design strategies, and ensuring projects meet their goals. I also value making decisions informed by data and insights.

I'm also exploring how Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Large Language Models (LLMs) can be thoughtfully integrated into design work.

Sharing Knowledge
